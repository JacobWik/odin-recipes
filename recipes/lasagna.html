Lasagna

Description

This Delicious lasagna recipe is not only vegan but also vegetarian.

Its filled with a lot of vitamins and minerals, no need to eat anything else!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta plates
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Parmesan Cheese

Ingredients

  1. make a roux with the milk and flour
  2. add milk and cheese to make bechamel
  3. add meat and mix and voila, you have lasagna
